Mr. Bean: The Animated Series is a British animated sitcom based on the live-action television sitcom of the same title, created by Rowan Atkinson and Richard Curtis. The series follows the misadventures of Mr. Bean (voiced by Atkinson), along with his teddy bear, Irma Gobb, and Mrs. Wicket, his landlady. The series originally aired from 2002 to 2004 and featured 52 episodes. The show returned in 2015 with two new series and a total of 78 episodes, featuring more dialogue than the original series. Mr. Bean is known for its physical comedy and silent humor, and the animated series continues to entertain audiences of all ages.
